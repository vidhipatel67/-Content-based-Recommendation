<p>The interface methods and interfaces are always <code><a href="/wiki/Java_Programming/Keywords/public" title="Java Programming/Keywords/public"><font style="color:DodgerBlue4; text-decoration: none; font-weight: bold;">public</font></a></code>. You do not need to specify the access modifier. It will default to <code><a href="/wiki/Java_Programming/Keywords/public" title="Java Programming/Keywords/public"><font style="color:DodgerBlue4; text-decoration: none; font-weight: bold;">public</font></a></code>. For clarity it is considered a good practice to put the <code><a href="/wiki/Java_Programming/Keywords/public" title="Java Programming/Keywords/public"><font style="color:DodgerBlue4; text-decoration: none; font-weight: bold;">public</font></a></code> keyword.</p>
