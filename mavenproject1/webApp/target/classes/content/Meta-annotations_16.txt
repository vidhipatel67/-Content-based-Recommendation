<p>The <code>@Target</code> meta-annotation determines what may be marked by the annotation. The <code>value</code> attribute is one or more of the <code>java.lang.annotation.ElementType</code> enum constants. Those constants are <code>ElementType.ANNOTATION_TYPE</code>, <code>CONSTRUCTOR</code>, <code>FIELD</code>, <code>LOCAL_VARIABLE</code>, <code>METHOD</code>, <code>PACKAGE</code>, <code>PARAMETER</code>, and <code>TYPE</code>.</p>
