<p>The success of the Java platform and the concepts of the write once, run anywhere principle has led to the development of similar frameworks and platforms. Most notable of these is the Microsoft's <a href="//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/.NET_Framework" class="extiw" title="w:.NET Framework">.NET framework</a> and its open-source equivalent <a href="//en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mono_(software)" class="extiw" title="w:Mono (software)">Mono</a>.</p>
