<p>In the above example, a simple method is used to define precisely where to place the line on the Cartesian scale of the canvas. The <code style="border-bottom:dashed green 1px;">drawLine(int,int,int,int)</code> asks you to put four arguments, appearing in order, the <b>x1 coordinate</b>, the <b>y1 coordinate</b>, the <b>x2 coordinate</b> and the <b>y2 coordinate</b>. Running the program will show a simple black line diagonally going across the canvas.<br style="clear:both;"></p>
