<p>The <code>final</code> keyword forbids to overwrite the method in a subclass. It is useless if the class is already final and a private method is implicitly <code><a href="/wiki/Java_Programming/Keywords/final" title="Java Programming/Keywords/final"><font style="color:DodgerBlue4; text-decoration: none; font-weight: bold;">final</font></a></code>. A final method cannot be <code><a href="/wiki/Java_Programming/Keywords/abstract" title="Java Programming/Keywords/abstract"><font style="color:DodgerBlue4; text-decoration: none; font-weight: bold;">abstract</font></a></code>.</p>
