<p>Java does not have jump statements. The <code><a href="/wiki/Java_Programming/Keywords/goto" title="Java Programming/Keywords/goto"><font style="color:DodgerBlue4; text-decoration: none; font-weight: bold;">goto</font></a></code> is reserved, but not used. The <code><a href="/wiki/Java_Programming/Keywords/break" title="Java Programming/Keywords/break"><font style="color:DodgerBlue4; text-decoration: none; font-weight: bold;">break</font></a></code> statement is close to a jump statetement, but the destination of the jump is well defined. It breaks/jumps out from a loop, or a <code><a href="/wiki/Java_Programming/Keywords/switch" title="Java Programming/Keywords/switch"><font style="color:DodgerBlue4; text-decoration: none; font-weight: bold;">switch</font></a></code> statement.</p>
